About the company

A technology and media company headquartered in Warsaw, Poland

forBET Media is an independent product and engineering team building digital media applications, content systems, and audience platforms for the modern sports and information landscape.

01 — Overview

Company overview

forBET Media is a Poland-based company operating at the intersection of mobile technology and digital publishing. Our team combines product design, software engineering, and editorial tooling expertise to create applications and platforms that serve sports fans, content publishers, and information communities.

We focus on long-term product work rather than one-off contracts. Each product we develop is engineered for reliability, accessibility, and responsible content delivery across global audiences.

02 — Mission

Our mission

To build digital media products that are fast, trustworthy, and genuinely useful — helping audiences stay informed about the sports, teams, and topics they follow, and helping publishers operate modern content businesses with the tools they need.

We believe great media technology should be invisible: clear interfaces, dependable performance, and respectful handling of every user's data.

03 — What we build

What we build

Our product work covers several connected areas of the digital media stack:

  • Mobile applications for sports news, schedules, results, and editorial content.
  • Content aggregation systems that bring multiple feeds and sources into a single, clean experience.
  • Fan engagement and companion tools that enhance the experience around live events.
  • Analytics and editorial dashboards that help media teams understand and serve their audiences.
  • Publishing infrastructure that supports modern, mobile-first delivery.
04 — Principles

Operating principles

  • ✓
    Quality over volume

    We focus on a small number of products and deliver them carefully — performance, accessibility, and clarity are non-negotiable.

  • ✓
    Privacy by default

    We collect only what is necessary, communicate clearly with users about how data is handled, and follow EU data protection standards.

  • ✓
    Responsible content

    Our products present information factually. We do not produce, host, or distribute restricted or misleading content.

  • ✓
    Transparent operations

    We maintain clear company information, accessible support channels, and documented compliance practices for partners and platforms.

05 — Location

Poland & EU presence

forBET Media is registered and operates from Warsaw, Poland — an established hub for European technology and media companies. Our European base gives us a regulated environment for business operations, strong access to engineering talent, and direct alignment with GDPR and EU digital standards.

From Warsaw, we collaborate with partners, contributors, and clients across Europe and beyond, supporting product work in multiple markets while maintaining a single, accountable corporate presence.
