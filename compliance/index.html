Standards & responsibility

Operating with transparency, accountability, and clear standards

forBET Media follows established practices for corporate transparency, content responsibility, data protection, and platform compliance — across every product and service we deliver.

01 — Transparency

Company transparency

forBET Media operates as a registered business entity in Warsaw, Poland. We maintain accurate company information, an accessible business contact, and clear documentation of our team, services, and product activities.

Verified company information is available on request for partners, distribution platforms, and official inquiries.

02 — Content standards

Content standards

All content delivered through our products is informational, editorial, or media-focused. Content is reviewed for accuracy, clarity, and appropriate context. We avoid sensationalism and present information factually.

Our products do not host, promote, or facilitate restricted, misleading, or otherwise non-compliant material. We maintain editorial guidelines that apply across every surface we operate.

03 — User privacy

User privacy

We treat user privacy as a foundational requirement. Across our products we apply data minimization, clear consent mechanics, and accessible user controls. Individuals can request access to or deletion of their data at any time via our published contact channels.

Detailed practices are outlined in our Privacy Policy.

04 — Data protection

Data protection

Our data protection practices are aligned with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other applicable EU frameworks. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest, access is restricted to authorized personnel, and retention policies are documented and enforced.

Where third-party services are used, we evaluate their data processing terms and limit data sharing to what is strictly necessary for the service to function.

05 — App distribution

App distribution responsibility

When we distribute applications through third-party stores and platforms, we follow the published policies of those platforms. We provide complete and accurate listing information, clear descriptions of features, and appropriate disclosures about data handling and permissions.

We submit only applications that comply with platform policies and applicable law in the markets where they are distributed.

06 — Restricted content

No restricted or misleading content

Our products do not contain, facilitate, or promote content that violates platform policies or applicable law. This includes — but is not limited to — restricted financial activities, misleading information, infringing material, and content unsuitable for general audiences.

Any concerns regarding content in our products can be raised through the contact channels published on this site. Reports are reviewed promptly and addressed in line with our editorial and compliance practices.

Ongoing review

Compliance is a continuous practice

We review our policies, content standards, and technical practices regularly. As regulations, platform policies, and audience expectations evolve, our procedures evolve with them.

Documented policies

Our internal policies are written down, versioned, and reviewed on a regular schedule — covering content, data, and operational practice.

Verified information

Company information presented on this site is accurate and consistent with the records held in our registered jurisdiction.

Open contact

Inquiries from regulators, partners, distribution platforms, and individuals are answered via clear, monitored business channels.

Questions about our compliance practices?

We're happy to provide additional information to partners, platforms, and regulatory bodies on request.
