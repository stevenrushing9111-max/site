Our product categories

Informational media products built for sports audiences

Our product portfolio covers mobile applications and web experiences focused on news, schedules, content discovery, and audience tooling — engineered for reliability and ease of use.

Mobile

Sports News Applications

Mobile-first news experiences focused on coverage, schedules, and editorial stories. Designed for quick daily reading, clear navigation, and respectful notifications.

Web & mobile

Live Content Hubs

Real-time editorial surfaces that aggregate live updates, schedules, and informational content during major sporting moments — without distractions.

Companion

Fan Companion Apps

Companion experiences for following favorite teams, leagues, and competitions — with curated content, calendars, and personalized informational feeds.

Internal

Analytics Dashboards

Internal dashboards that help editorial and product teams understand audience patterns, performance, and content reach — built with privacy-respecting analytics.

CMS / Tools

Publisher Tools

Editorial tooling, scheduling utilities, and publishing helpers that streamline how teams prepare, review, and distribute content across digital surfaces.

Platform

Content Delivery Platform

The underlying delivery layer that powers our front-end products — fast APIs, cached media assets, and reliable infrastructure across the EU and beyond.

Product disclaimer. All forBET Media products are strictly informational and media-focused. They are designed to deliver news, editorial content, schedules, and audience experiences. Our products do not facilitate, host, or enable any financial transactions or restricted activities. Content is presented for general information and entertainment purposes only.

Product principles

How we approach product work

Every product we ship starts from the same foundation — clear purpose, careful design, and respect for the audience using it.

Fast by default

Every screen, every interaction, every feed is optimized to load quickly and respond instantly across modern devices and networks.

Accessible to everyone

Inclusive design choices, scalable typography, sensible color contrast, and screen-reader friendly markup are non-negotiable.

Privacy respected

We minimize data collection by design and provide clear controls, consent flows, and transparent practices to every user.
